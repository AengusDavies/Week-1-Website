Readyreadr

An ebook/audiobook website with a difference.

Website Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide a variety of ebooks for users to read, with the option for the user to listen to it as an audiobook. The user will be able to not only choose from a variety of preset voices to listen to, but will also have the ability to customise the voice to any that they wish to listen to via an AI-powered voice modifier. The user would enter in the name and origin of any person or character (be it real or fictional) that is then used to augment the listening experience to their liking

Business Goal

Readyreadr is a startup business that provides a more modern take on the reading of ebooks and the listening of audiobooks. The business aims to capitalise on the use of Artificial Intelligence by allowing users to modify their audiobook listening experience to whatever voice they would like it to be, regardless of the book that they are listening to. Our ideal customer would be an individual who likes audiobooks, and would be interested in a variety of listening options that is only limited to one's imagination.

Business Promotion

Our preferred method for the promotion of this venture will be through Search Engine Optimisation. By improving the website ranking in search engines, the business shall be afforded an inexpensive means for customer acquisition.
